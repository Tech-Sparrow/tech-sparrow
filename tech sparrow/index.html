About

Tech Sparrow is the home for experiments that graduate into real products: mobile and web apps, browser extensions, Flutter packages, and utilities you can fork or ship as-is. We care about maintainable architecture, honest documentation, and interfaces that stay out of your way.

Whether you need a sharper developer workflow, a calmer end-user experience, or a reference implementation you can learn from, we lean on modern stacks and ship in the open so the community can benefit.
